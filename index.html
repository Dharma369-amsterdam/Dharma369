Corporate Subscription Model

We work directly with businesses, offering **monthly wellness subscriptions** instead of individual bookings. Our programs ensure employees have access to **stress-relief sessions during work hours**, boosting overall efficiency and well-being.

How It Works

✅ **Companies sign up for a wellness plan** (customized for team size).
✅ **Employees book short sessions** (meditation, massage, or sensory tanks).
✅ **No payments on-site** – everything is included in the corporate plan.
✅ **Sessions last 30 minutes**, ensuring efficiency without disrupting work.
