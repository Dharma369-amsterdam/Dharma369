About Us

Our methodology is backed by scientific studies showing that guided meditation can **reduce workplace stress by up to 40%** and **improve cognitive function by 20%**. Investing in employee wellness is not just a benefit—it’s a necessity. In the Netherlands, stress-related absenteeism costs businesses **billions annually**, with **burnout accounting for over 20% of long-term sick leave cases**. Studies indicate that companies investing in structured wellness programs see a **30% reduction in employee sick days** and **a 25% boost in productivity**.
